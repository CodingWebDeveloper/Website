Nike - Official partner of Kylian Mbappé since 2006

KYLIAN MBAPPE, AMBASSADOR FOR HUBLOT

A major player in the world of football, the Swiss watch-maker is proud to support Kylian Mbappé, a champion who embodies the dream and victory! “Hublot embodies this fusion of innovation, new technologies and materials, which greatly appeals to me. They unconditionally support my sport and are associated with my finest moments in football, so I am proud to become the first active footballer to act as an ambassador for the brand!”

Kylian Mbappé is the face of FIFA 21 around the world.

“Being on the cover of FIFA is a dream come true. From my time at Bondy in Clairefontaine to my World Cup victory, it’s another great milestone. I’ve been playing FIFA since I was a kid, and I’m thrilled to represent a new generation of footballers and to join other great footballers who share this honour.” This is the first time in the history of the game that a player has been represented alone on the jacket to personify the three versions of the game.
